1. Introduction

We strive to provide the best possible service experience. If you are not satisfied with the services purchased from our website, this Return & Refund Policy explains your options.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds for IT services are available under the following conditions:

  • The service has not yet been delivered or started.

Refunds are not provided for completed and delivered services unless otherwise specified in a written agreement.

3. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at contact@codecrux.com within 14 days of the purchase date. Include your order details and a description of the issue. We will review your request and notify you of the status of your refund within [Insert Review Timeframe, e.g., "7 business days"].

4. Partial Refunds

In some cases, partial refunds may be issued at our discretion if a portion of the service has been completed and delivered, and the remaining work cannot be completed as agreed.

5. Service Cancellations

If you wish to cancel a service before it begins, please notify us as soon as possible. Cancellations made prior to service initiation may qualify for a full refund, minus any transaction fees that may apply.

6. Exceptions

We do not provide refunds for services rendered or for custom software development and consulting work once it has been started or completed, unless there is a specific provision in the service agreement.

7. Dispute Resolution

In the case of a dispute, we will work with you to resolve the issue amicably. If a resolution cannot be reached, you may be eligible for arbitration or other dispute resolution methods as stipulated in our terms of service.
