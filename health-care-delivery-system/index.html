FEATURES:

1.Easy registration and login

Registration and login are the first steps of interaction with patients and doctors.

2.Profile access and modification:

Creating a profile should be easy and handy. Users should be allowed to upload their photos and any other information that they want to share.

3. Searching and consulting Doctors:

The best healthcare app should have a Smart filter to search and filter the doctors based on their specialization, experience, reviews, consulting fee, education, location, etc.

4. Selecting Doctors:

Patients should be able to choose and select the Doctors according to their search results after the filters.

5. Appointment management:

The doctors should get authorization to accept or deny consultation requests if, for some reason, they do not wish to see the patient or the timings are not available.

6. Make your website mobile friendly

The mobile device is the new gatekeeper, in essence. It must be easy to use on the smallest screen available if you want your patients, prospective patients, and staff to use your website.

7. Create a wide range of content for patient and other health care end users

The need for it is already there. People from a reputable source they trust want to learn more about their healthcare. The more the customer is educated, the lower the total healthcare rate. Cost reductions that do not cut care are necessary with burgeoning expenses.

8. Personalise your website experience

Imagine, for instance, running a small practise with a customer portal. To make a daily appointment, a patient who is trying to stop smoking logs in. That could be an opportunity to provide them with content from inside your system about smoking cessation

9. Develop secure communication channels

Private companies , particularly service providers, have gone beyond simple telephone support to include other platforms such as ticketing systems, live chat, chatbots, and aid powered by social media. The same would need to be achieved by healthcare professionals.
